Data Set

Novel Corona Virus (COVID-19) epidemiological data since 22 January 2020. The data is compiled by the Johns Hopkins University Center for Systems Science and Engineering (JHU CCSE) from various sources including the World Health Organization (WHO), DXY.cn. Overall, original dataset has 9 columns and over 21914 rows, below you can find important columns that were used on this website's visualizations and their detailed description:

  1. Province/State: Infected Country's Province.
  2. Country/Region: Infected Country.
  3. Confirmed: Number of Confirmed Cases.
  4. Recovered: Number of Recovered Cases.
  5. Deaths: Number of Deaths.
